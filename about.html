About

Keraunoi

(kee-ruh-noy) / (keh-ruh-noy)

Hey there! I'm Keraunoi, a digital artist. I love drawing animals and the sort, especially hybrids and fictional creatures.

I've been around for over a decade in various online spaces, but I really started properly posting art in 2015 on Instagram. At the start I still went by the username "IceBlue," the same user I used in online games like Feral Heart (if you remember me, hi!).

If you want to contact me, you can message me on my socials linked below, message on Discord @keraunoi, or send an email to thekeraunoi@gmail.com.
