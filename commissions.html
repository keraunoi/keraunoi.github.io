Commissions

I offer character art commissions for both feral and anthro animals (I have more experience with feral characters). You can check out my social media for examples of characters and art I've drawn so far.

Before commissioning me, I ask that you read through my Terms of Service where I outline important conditions regarding payments, refunds, communication, what I allow in terms of themes or characters, and more.

Commission Status

Open

At the moment, I don't have any schedule for when I open commissions. I just fill a random number of slots, work on them, and then open commissions again (unless I don't have time for more).

If you want to get notified any time my commissions open, you can join my Discord Server, linked here. Alternatively, you can just check back here every now and then or watch for updates I might post on social media.

Prices & Info

Price Details

Most prices listed for commissions are minimum or "starting prices". The cost of a commission can increase based on the content and complexity of the art, aka how long it takes me to draw it (this applies to both characters and backgrounds).

Discounts

Returning customer If you have commissioned me before, you can get a 5% discount on your commission.
Mass commission If you order 5 or more commissions at the same time, you get a 5% discount on the total cost of all commissions.
