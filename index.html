Welcome

My name is Keraunoi, and welcome to my website! I needed a place to store all of my commission info and other stuff, so I made this website. I got tired of trying to build something with Wix and decided to just learn HTML and CSS (still haven't really figured out JS...).

Updates

A small update log of recent changes and news

03/07/2024

  • New website, the one you're on right now!
  • Big updates to commissions:
    • Divided categories into "Regular Art" and "Reference Sheets." Customs and redesigns are now available as part of ref sheet commissions. Both categories have "Chibi" style options.
    • Some commissions now allow backgrounds! I have also added/changed levels: head, bust, half, and full.
    • All prices have been increased since the last time I did commissions. I may keep adjusting them in the future.
    • All application forms have been replaced by a single simple form to make things cleaner and easier. the form now only asks for basic information and leaves room for more creative freedom for your commission.
  • New username and accounts:
    • My new username is Keraunoi! If you recognized the art/characters and art I post but were confused by the different user, that's why. I wanted to start over with a more unique username. I also don't want anyone IRL who knows my old user finding me again.
    • I plan on permanently leaving Instagram. The updates from the past few years killed my account; I also just hate the app and want to delete any accounts connected to Facebook.
    • You can now find me on FurAffinity and Tumblr! If you have any other good platform suggestions, please tell me. Deviantart is plagued with AI, and a lot of other paltforms seem to be dying or just don't have that big of an audience.
