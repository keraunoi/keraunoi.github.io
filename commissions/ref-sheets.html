Reference Sheets

A basic ref sheet by default includes a full-body, headshot, and color palette.

Customization

Reference sheets can be customized however you'd like. For example, adding/removing views of the character or objects/accessories (line art and views that are flipped/copied cost less). You can also add:

  • other characters/figures (you can add chibis to standard sheets)
  • text, labels, and descriptions
  • extra pages/files to fit more content

Customs & Redesigns

You can get a custom character designed for you, or have an existing character redesigned. For customs, you can give descriptions, mood boards, references, or sketches. Redesigns can be as simple as changing a few details and colors, or completely redoing the design and changing the species.
